Create a new contact:

Use the toggle button 'Add a new contact' to display and close the form windows.
Then, fill the form and clic on the 'Save' button.
A clic on the 'Close' button will clear and close the form.

Delete a contact:

Clic carefully the 'Delete' button on the same row as the contact to be deleted.
Please, notice that there is no confirmation dialog needed...

Update an existent contact:

Clip the 'Update' button to display form windows and see it filled of the contact information.
Modify the contact and clic on the 'Save' button.
A clic on the 'Close' button will clear and close the form.
